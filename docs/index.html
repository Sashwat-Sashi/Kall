Our Mission

Kall (meaning “stone” in Marathi) aims to document and promote outdoor and indoor climbing across Mumbai and Maharashtra. From local basalt crags to emerging gyms, we’re creating an open, community-driven climbing resource.

Outdoor Climbing

Explore Maharashtra’s best crags — Duke’s Nose, Kanheri, Mumbra, Manori, and more. We’re curating topos, access info, and safety notes to help climbers explore responsibly.

Indoor Climbing

Track climbing gyms and training centers around Mumbai. We support grassroots gyms that introduce new climbers to the sport.

Coming soon — training guides, gear rental spots, and route databases.
